Our 7-day money-back guarantee.

Full refund within 7 days if the signal is not what you expected.

Last updated: June 17, 2026

At StreamGodz, we stand behind the quality of our service with a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not fully satisfied with your subscription, you can request a full refund within 7 days of purchase — no hassle, no long forms.

1. The 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

You may request a full refund within 7 calendar days from the date of purchase if:

  • You experience persistent technical issues we cannot resolve
  • The service does not work in your region
  • The content does not match what was advertised

To qualify, you must contact our support team first to give us a reasonable opportunity to fix any issues.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team using the contact info in your activation email.
  2. Include your order number, the email used to purchase, and the reason for the refund.
  3. Allow us up to 48 hours to review your request and provide a decision.
  4. Once approved, refunds are processed within 5–10 business days back to your original payment method.

3. What Is NOT Eligible for a Refund

The following are not eligible for a refund:

  • Requests made more than 7 days after purchase
  • Accounts terminated for violating our Terms of Use (e.g. credential sharing beyond device limits, reselling content)
  • Service disruptions caused by your internet provider, hardware, or factors outside our control
  • Issues you did not first report to our support team
  • Renewal payments after the initial 7-day period

4. Partial Refunds

If you used the service for several days before requesting a refund and there is no service-quality issue, we may offer a pro-rated partial refund at our discretion.

5. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback through your bank or payment processor without first contacting us, your account will be immediately suspended. We always prefer to resolve issues directly — please contact support before disputing a charge.

6. Cryptocurrency Payments

For payments made via cryptocurrency, refunds are issued in the same cryptocurrency, calculated at the exchange rate at the time of the original payment. Due to crypto volatility, the dollar value of your refund may differ from the original payment.

7. Renewals

StreamGodz subscriptions do not auto-renew by default. You must manually purchase a new subscription when your current one expires. Renewal payments are subject to the same 7-day refund window.

8. Cancellation vs. Refund

Cancelling your subscription stops future renewals but does not refund the current billing period. To request a refund of an existing subscription, follow the steps in section 2.

9. Contact

Refund questions or requests? Contact our support team through the channels in your activation email or on our support page. We respond within minutes during business hours.
