CH.200 · ACTIVATION GUIDES

Install StreamGodz on any device.

Pick your device below. Each guide walks you through setup in under 5 minutes — no technical experience required.

DEV.001 · AMAZON FIRESTICK

Amazon Firestick setup

Recommended app: IPTV Smarters Pro or TiviMate

01

Enable Apps from Unknown Sources

Go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options. Turn ON Apps from Unknown Sources.

02

Install the Downloader App

Go to the home screen, click the search icon, type Downloader. Install from Amazon's app store.

03

Download IPTV Smarters Pro

Open Downloader, enter the URL from your activation email. Press Go, then Install.

04

Enter Your StreamGodz Credentials

Open IPTV Smarters Pro → Login with Xtream Codes API. Enter username, password, and server URL from your email.

05

Begin Transmission

Browse 45,000+ live channels, VOD, and series. Activation complete.

DEV.002 · ANDROID

Android setup

Recommended app: IPTV Smarters Pro

01

Open Google Play Store

Search for IPTV Smarters Pro and install. (Free)

02

Launch the App

Accept the user agreement and grant requested permissions.

03

Add Your StreamGodz Account

Select Login with Xtream Codes API. Enter credentials from your activation email.

04

Begin Transmission

Channels, movies, and series load automatically. Tap any channel to stream.

DEV.003 · iOS

iPhone / iPad / Apple TV setup

Recommended apps: IPTV Smarters Pro or IBO Player

01

Open the App Store

Search for IPTV Smarters Player. Tap Get to install.

02

Open the App

Tap Accept on the user agreement.

03

Add Your Subscription

Choose Login with Xtream Codes API. Enter credentials from your activation email.

04

Stream Anywhere

Library syncs automatically. Watch on the go — subscription works worldwide.

DEV.004 · SAMSUNG / LG SMART TV

Smart TV setup

Recommended app: Smart IPTV or Smart STB

01

Open Your TV's App Store

Samsung: Samsung Apps store. LG: LG Content Store.

02

Install Smart IPTV

Search for Smart IPTV or SS IPTV. Install the free app.

03

Note Your MAC Address

Open the app. The TV's MAC address is displayed. Send this MAC to support via your activation email contact info.

04

Activation Complete

Support links your subscription to your TV's MAC. Restart the app — channels load.

DEV.005 · MAC / WINDOWS

Mac & Windows PC setup

Recommended player: VLC Media Player (free, all platforms)

01

Download VLC

Go to videolan.org and download VLC for your OS. Install it.

02

Open VLC

From the menu, choose Media → Open Network Stream (Windows) or File → Open Network (Mac).

03

Enter Your M3U URL

Paste the M3U URL from your activation email, click Play. The full channel list loads.

04

Optional: Save Playlist

Save the M3U as a file so you don't re-enter the URL each session.

DEV.006 · MAG / ENIGMA2

MAG Box / Enigma2 setup

For MAG 254/322/420/520 and Enigma2 boxes (Dreambox, VU+, etc.)

01

Find Your MAC Address

On your MAG box: Settings → System Settings → Servers → Portals. MAC shown in device settings.

02

Send Us Your MAC Address

Send the MAC to support via the contact info in your activation email.

03

We'll Send Your Portal URL

Enter the portal URL into Portal 1 URL in your MAG portal settings.

04

Reboot & Begin Transmission

Restart your MAG box. All StreamGodz channels and VOD will be available.
